HOW IT WORKS

From goal to shipped

The NS-System loop runs autonomously — you set the north star, it figures out what to build next.

Define a North Star

Add a project with a metric and target. Sub-stars break the goal into measurable components.

Add milestones

Type what needs to be done. Stones are auto-acked and appear in review. You queue them — Claude works them.

Execute

Press Execute (or click +msg to queue and fire). Claude spawns a session, reads the stone queue, and works through them sequentially.

Confirm

Each completed stone enters pending_confirmation. You review the gap-closure note and confirm or reopen. Done stones retire to the table.
