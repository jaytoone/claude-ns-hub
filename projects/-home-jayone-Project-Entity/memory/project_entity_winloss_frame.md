Entity's core cognitive frame is **win/lose (승패), not success/fail**.

**Why:** 재원 realized that the human world — politics, business, human relations, and more — is fundamentally game-like. Entity should see situations the way a strategically-aware human does: who comes out ahead, relative positioning, reputation across repeated encounters. Task-completion framing ("did it work?") misses what actually matters in human situations ("who gained, who lost ground?").

**How to apply:**
- all reasoning inside Entity (framework nodes, weights, episodic memory, developmental change) should evaluate interactions through positional/relational win-lose, not absolute correctness
- reinforcement and learning = positional learning (what stance/lens let me come out ahead)
- memory organized as episodes of win/lose, not logs of task outcomes
- even "solo" situations (reading reality alone) are still framed as game-like (against ambiguity, against past self, against what reality demanded)
